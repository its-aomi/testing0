Leave a Comment / By Your EDM Staff / July 27, 2012

MAKE SURE TO BOOKMARK THIS PAGE AS WE ARE ALWAYS UPDATING

Scroll down the page to get all the set download links for Avicii, Fatboy Slim, Carl Cox and more!

Perhaps the world’s most extravagantly decorated dance music festival, Tomorrowland is now live from Belgium! Featuring many of the biggest names in dance music, like Alesso, Avicii, John Digweed, Zedd, Gabriel & Dresden, Hardwell, Porter Robinson, Ferry Corsten, Tommy Trash, W&W, Gareth Emery, and Gregori Klosman With the main stage being streamed all weekend, you can rave at your desk even if you live halfway across the world. Check out the live stream below, and also the recap video from last year to get a glimpse of the incredible stage setups and art installations present at Tomorrowland!
